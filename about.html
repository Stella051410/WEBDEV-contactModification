Benefits of Watermelon

Watermelon isn't just a summer treat—it's a superfruit packed with benefits. With 92% water, it's perfect for staying hydrated on hot days. It's low in calories yet rich in vitamins A, C, and B6, plus powerful antioxidants like lycopene that support heart health. Watermelon also aids digestion, boosts skin and hair health, and makes for a sweet, guilt-free snack. Delicious and nutritious, it's the ultimate fruit for sunny days!
